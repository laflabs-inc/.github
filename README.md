# LafLabs

> **Technology that disappears into the experience.**

LafLabs is a software and infrastructure company focused on building simple, reliable, and trustworthy digital systems.

We create the foundational technology behind products — identity, payments, cloud infrastructure, developer tools, and services that reduce complexity for both users and businesses.

Our goal is simple:

**Build technology people can rely on without having to think about it.**

---

## What we build

### Laf ID
Unified identity and authentication infrastructure.

Designed around modern standards such as OAuth 2.0 and OpenID Connect, Laf ID provides a consistent foundation for authentication, account management, identity verification, and service integration across the LafLabs ecosystem.

### Laf Pay
Payment infrastructure for LafLabs products and external services.

Laf Pay is being designed as a secure and developer-friendly payment platform with consistent APIs, unified billing experiences, and extensible financial infrastructure.

### LafDock
Cloud infrastructure built for modern workloads.

LafDock provides compute, hosting, networking, and infrastructure services with a focus on simplicity, automation, and predictable operations.

---

## Our principles

### Simplicity
Complex systems should not create complex experiences.

### Reliability
Infrastructure should work consistently, especially when users depend on it most.

### Security
Secure defaults, least privilege, and defense in depth are fundamental requirements.

### Scalability
We design systems that can grow without requiring constant reinvention.

### Consistency
Products should feel like parts of one coherent platform, not disconnected services.

### User-first design
Technology exists to remove friction, not add more of it.

---

## Engineering at LafLabs

We value:

- Clear and maintainable architecture
- Secure-by-default systems
- Open standards and interoperable APIs
- Automation over repetitive manual operations
- Observability and operational transparency
- Readability over cleverness
- Long-term sustainability over short-term convenience

We prefer practical engineering principles such as **KISS**, **DRY**, **SOLID**, and **YAGNI**, applying abstraction only when it creates real value.
